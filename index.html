📌 The Problem

Gen Z learners are disengaged by old-school, textbook-style language apps.
They crave TikTok-speed content, social learning, and real-time feedback.
Traditional education can't keep up with the demands of digital-native students.

💡 Our Solution

CheetahLearn is the first AI-powered English app built for Gen Z.
Learners challenge each other, solve GPT-generated quizzes, and hear answers through TTS.
It’s social, gamified, personalized—and always fun.

🤖 GPT-Based Problem Generation

Our GPT-powered backend generates unlimited, personalized English content based on:

  • Quiz Types – MCQ, short answers, comprehension
  • Difficulty Levels – 6 tiers, from kids to experts
  • Multi-language Interface – KR, JP, ES, more
  • Audio Output – Native-like TTS voice
  • Real-Time Generation & Delivery
  • Smart Analytics – Know what to improve

📱 Social Feed

Post your scores, leave emoji reactions, comment on friends' progress, and compete in daily challenges—CheetahLearn turns your language journey into a social game feed.
